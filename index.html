This website is a tribute to Budots, a grassroots electronic dance genre that originated from Davao City, Philippines. DJ Love (also known as Sherwin Tuna) was one of the genre's earliest pioneers, producing Budots music from his internet cafe starting in the 2000s.

The background uses YouTube Trends data to visualize interest in Budots over time. The outer circle represents Budots' popularity in the Philippines since 2008, while the inner circle shows interest in the United States. In August 2024, Budots became viral on TikTok.

Budots is rooted in the creativity of low-income Filipino communities, produced on pirated FL Studio software and blasted from street parties across the Philippines. Despite its cultural significance, Budots has often been dismissed due to classist attitudes and a broader bias towards Western artistic standards. Yet, Budots has already proven its mainstream appeal. Deeply infused with a DIY spirit, Budots challenges conventional definitions of art and is a testament to Filipino ingenuity.
